#growlab

Grow your own with the Raspberry Pi for less than 30 dollars

#growlab is a community project to encourage people around the world to grow their own food and plants through the use of technology.

Everyone is welcome to join and you may even win a prize in our timelapse contest.

Frequently asked questions

Who should take part?
growlab combines science, technology and nature. It's about community and learning, rather than about being a gardening fanatic or someone with a huge back yard. You just need a few simple components, a windowsill and an Internet connection. The rest is up to you.
What do I need to take part?
You'll need a Raspberry Pi Zero and any compatible camera module to take part in the contest, however you can also try out the data logger without a camera.
How much does it cost?
You can get started for around 30 USD, but if you have the equipment already, it may cost you nothing.
Who is providing prizes?
OpenFaaS Ltd and Pimoroni will provide prizes for the community.
What experiments can you take part in?
We have a timelapse, live-preview and data-logger available as open-source components. You can also make your own experiments and take things further with moisture sensors and water pumps.
